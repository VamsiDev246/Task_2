Salaar

Salaar tells a thrilling story about power struggles and revenge. The main characters are Devaratha, played by Prabhas, and his childhood friend Vardharaja Mannar, played by Prithviraj Sukumaran. Prabhas, as Salaar, deals with the challenges of his character, guided by a strong sense of purpose.

Cast:

  1. Prabhas
  2. Prashanth Neel
  3. Shruthi Hassan
  4. Prithviraj Sukumaran
